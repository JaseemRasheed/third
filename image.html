LIONEL MESSI

The English Wikipedia is, along with the Simple English Wikipedia, one of two English-language editions of Wikipedia, an online encyclopedia. It was founded on 15 January 2001 as Wikipedia's first edition and, as of 6 July 2022, has the most articles of any edition, at 6,534,686.[1] As of July 2022, 11% of articles in all Wikipedias belong to the English-language edition; this share was more than 50% in 2003.[2][3] The edition's one-billionth edit was made on 13 January 2021.[4]
